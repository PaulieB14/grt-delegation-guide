01What is Delegation?

üí°
When you delegate GRT to an indexer, your tokens enter a shared pool. You no longer hold GRT directly ‚Äî you hold shares in that indexer's pool. Think of it like depositing into a vault. As the indexer earns rewards, the vault grows, and your shares become worth more.

Exchange Ratetokens √∑ shares ‚Äî increases over time as rewards flow in

03What Happens When You Delegate

Step 1 You delegate 10,000 GRT

The pool already has other delegators and accumulated rewards:

Pool BEFORE:
  tokens:  500,000 GRT
  shares:  400,000
  rate:    1.25 GRT per share

You receive shares at the current rate:

Your shares = 10,000 √∑ 1.25 = 8,000 shares
Pool AFTER:
  tokens:  510,000 GRT  (+10,000 from you)
  shares:  408,000       (+8,000 for you)
  rate:    still 1.25

Step 2 Indexer earns rewards

The indexer submits a Proof of Indexing (POI). 5,000 GRT in rewards are minted and deposited to the pool:

Pool AFTER rewards:
  tokens:  515,000 GRT  (+5,000 rewards)
  shares:  408,000       (unchanged!)
  rate:    1.2623
Your position:
  balance = 8,000 √ó 1.2623 = 10,098.04 GRT
  rewards = +98.04 GRT

You did nothing. The pool grew. Your shares are now worth more. This is autocompounding.

Step 3 Six months later

Pool:
  tokens:  560,000 GRT
  shares:  408,000
  rate:    1.3725

Your position:
  balance = 8,000 √ó 1.3725 = 10,980.39 GRT
  rewards = +980.39 GRT (+9.8%)

Your 10,000 GRT grew to 10,980 GRT without any action. The exchange rate did all the work ‚Äî each share just kept becoming worth more GRT as rewards flowed in.

04When Others Delegate or Undelegate

You're not alone in the pool. Other delegators joining or leaving affects the dynamics. Here's what actually happens:

üìâ
Someone delegates a large amount (Dilution)

A whale delegates 500,000 GRT into the same pool. They receive new shares at the current exchange rate. The pool's tokens and shares both increase proportionally.

BEFORE whale delegates:
  pool tokens:  515,000  |  pool shares:  408,000
  your shares: 8,000 = 1.96% of pool

Whale deposits 500,000 GRT ‚Üí gets 396,194 new shares

AFTER whale delegates:
  pool tokens:  1,015,000  |  pool shares:  804,194
  your shares: 8,000 = 0.99% of pool

Your GRT balance doesn't change. Your shares are still worth the same amount. But your percentage of the pool shrinks, so you earn a smaller slice of future rewards. The exchange rate stays the same ‚Äî no value is lost, only your future earning share decreases.

üìà
Someone undelegates a large amount (Concentration)

A large delegator exits the pool. Their shares are burned and tokens move to thawing.

BEFORE whale undelegates:
  your shares: 8,000 = 0.99% of pool

Whale undelegates 396,194 shares

AFTER whale undelegates:
  your shares: 8,000 = 1.96% of pool

Your balance still doesn't change. But now you own a bigger percentage of the pool, so you earn a larger slice of future rewards. This is a tailwind for remaining delegators.

üîë
The key takeaway

Other delegators joining or leaving never changes your current balance. The exchange rate ensures your shares are always worth the correct amount of GRT. What changes is your share of future rewards:

‚Ä¢ More delegators = your future reward slice gets smaller
‚Ä¢ Fewer delegators = your future reward slice gets bigger
‚Ä¢ The pool's exchange rate never goes down from delegation activity ‚Äî it only goes up from rewards

05The Three Layers of Rewards

üü¢
Realized Rewards
GRT you've fully withdrawn to your wallet by completing the undelegate ‚Üí thaw ‚Üí withdraw process. These are done ‚Äî the GRT is yours.
üü°
Unrealized ‚Äî Earned (On-Chain)
Rewards that have been minted as real GRT and deposited into the delegation pool. They exist in the contract right now. You can verify them on Arbiscan.

earned = (your_shares √ó pool_tokens √∑ pool_shares) - original_delegation

Unrealized ‚Äî Estimated (Pending)
Rewards accrued in the RewardsManager but NOT minted yet. They become real only when the indexer submits a POI. The Explorer includes these estimates in the "Unrealized" number.

08How to Withdraw (Undelegation)

  1. Undelegate ‚Äî Your shares move from active to thawing. A countdown begins.
  2. Wait for thawing period ‚Äî Tokens are locked and no longer earning rewards.
  3. Withdraw ‚Äî GRT transfers to your wallet. Now shows as "Realized" in Explorer.
‚ö†Ô∏è
Important: Once you undelegate, your tokens stop earning rewards during the thawing period. Only undelegate when you're ready to exit.

10FAQ

Why do I see unrealized rewards immediately after delegating?
The Explorer estimates pending rewards from the RewardsManager that haven't been minted yet. The contract itself would show ~0, but the Explorer adds the estimated portion.
Are my rewards compounding?
Yes, automatically. Rewards increase pool.tokens while your shares stay the same. Your larger balance earns proportionally more on the next distribution.
What determines how much reward I get?
Indexer performance ‚Äî POIs submitted, allocations active. Delegation fee cut ‚Äî the percentage the indexer shares with you. Your pool share ‚Äî your shares √∑ total shares.
Does someone else delegating reduce my balance?
No ‚Äî your GRT balance stays exactly the same. But your share of future rewards decreases because you now own a smaller percentage of the pool. Conversely, when someone undelegates, your future reward share increases.
Is there still a delegation tax?
No. The 0.5% tax from the legacy contract was removed in Horizon.
Contract balance vs Explorer balance?
Contract = earned rewards only (real GRT). Explorer = earned + estimated pending. The gap is typically small.
